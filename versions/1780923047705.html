How to save a PDF to a specific folder · how the outreach works
Save as PDF to your folder: on the Résumé or Cover-letter tab, click Save as PDF. Your browser's print dialog opens with a live preview — choose “Save as PDF” as the destination, then pick the exact folder. That dialog is your permission + location step.
Outreach (2 contacts / role): open the LinkedIn search links, pick the hiring manager + one team peer, send the pre-written connection note. After you apply, send the referral email (subject + body provided). Sending stays manual on purpose — it protects your accounts and gets far better reply rates than automation.
